Flexible Timing

Our Batch Timings are flexible in order to be convenient for the young students, graduates and working professionals. So you can complete your IT Training course along with college, studies or a full-time job.



Benefits of Flexible Timing

Students can come on week days as well as weekends for attending classes.

If a student of weekday’s batch is absent and missed its current topic he/she can come on week end batches to complete the topic and vice versa.

If you didn’t understand any topic you can ask for re – explanation anytime you want.

If your certain batch duration is completed and you want to re-attain classes for that particular course you can come and attain as many batches as want.

You can choose any batch between 9.30 AM to 7.30 PM. Special classes will be arranged for those who don’t prefer to come between 9.30 AM to 7.30 PM.

You can practice as much as you want in the Institute premises.

Students can come on week days as well as weekends for attending classes.

You can set your own batch timings as you feel you will comfortable with.
